    CINEMATOGRAPHER

    Professional Cinematographer and Filmmaker based in Cairo, Egypt

    With a rich tapestry of ten years in the film and TV industry, I bring a unique vision and storytelling prowess to every project.
    My journey began at The High Cinema Institute, specializing in Cinematography, where I honed my skills from Sep 2012 to Jul 2018. Complementing this, my education at Culture & Science {City Mass Communication} in Cairo, 6th of October City, from Aug 2006 to Sep 2010, laid the foundation for my artistic exploration.

    As a freelance Cinematographer and Filmmaker since 2012, I've been on a quest to capture moments that transcend the screen.
